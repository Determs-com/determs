# Sponsors

Determs is an open standard and an open-core engine for verifiable automated
decisions — built to stay neutral and vendor-independent, *including independent
of us*. Sponsorship keeps it that way: it funds the work without selling any
influence over it.

## How it works

Two simple levels, name-your-amount — any amount is welcome:

- **One-time** — a thank-you. That's it.
- **Recurring** — listed below, in order of joining (every recurring sponsor,
  whatever the amount). Stop anytime and you come off the list.

Sponsorship buys **recognition, never influence**: no priority support, no
roadmap votes, no "endorsed" or "certified" status. Conformance is something
anyone proves for themselves against the public
[test vectors](docs/spec/test-vectors/) — it is never granted, and never for
sale.
