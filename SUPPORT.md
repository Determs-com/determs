# Support

Determs is an open standard and an open-core reference implementation,
maintained by a very small team.

- **Documentation** — start with the [README](README.md) and the
  [Verifiable Decision Record spec](docs/spec/verifiable-decision-record-v0.md).
- **Bugs** — open an issue, but please read [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md)
  first: review is best-effort and may be slow.
- **Security** — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Do not use public issues.
- **Commercial support** — guaranteed support, the managed registry, and the
  compliance layer are commercial. See [determs.com](https://determs.com) or
  email contact@determs.com.

There is no support guarantee for the open-source components. They are
provided "as is" under Apache-2.0.
